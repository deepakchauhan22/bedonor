    Medical Emergency

    Many are fortunate enough to get any medical treatment required to fight their condition but there’s a many more who are not able to bear the expenses of modern treatments they might need to give themselves a chance to live a healthy life. People are often left exhausted of all their savings and worse when there’s none or insufficient insurance coverage. BeDonor could be that chance, that hope; when you can’t do it all by yourself, BeDonor will help you reach out to the people who can help. Start the fundraiser for Medical Emergencies and get help when you need it the most.
